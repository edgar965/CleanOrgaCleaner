Cleaning management for teams - Tasks, time tracking & communication

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FULL DESCRIPTION (max 4000 characters):
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CleanOrga is the professional app for cleaning teams and facility management. Manage cleaning tasks, track working hours, and communicate in real-time with your team.

FEATURES:

Task Management
- Daily overview of all assigned cleaning tasks
- Detailed task information with checklists
- Status tracking: Not started, In progress, Completed
- Create and manage your own tasks

Documentation
- Take photos before and after cleaning
- Report problems with description and image
- Add notes to each task
- Complete logging of all activities

Time Tracking
- Start and end work with a single tap
- Automatic calculation of working hours
- Overview of all work days

Communication
- Integrated team chat
- Real-time notifications
- Direct communication with management

Multilingual
- Available in: German, English, Spanish, Romanian, Polish, Russian, Ukrainian, Vietnamese

IDEAL FOR:
- Cleaning companies
- Facility management
- Hotels and vacation rentals
- Co-living spaces
- Property management

The app works with the CleanOrga server system. Contact us for access.
